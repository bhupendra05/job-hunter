# Thumbnail / hero image prompt — Job Hunter v1.0

Use these with any image generator (Midjourney, DALL·E, Imagen, Flux, etc.).
Target a 1200×627 LinkedIn link-preview ratio (or 1280×720 for a YouTube-style thumb).

---

## Prompt A — Clean product hero (recommended for LinkedIn)

> A modern, clean SaaS hero illustration for an open-source job-search tool called
> "Job Hunter". Left side: bold sans-serif title "JOB HUNTER" in deep slate (#0f172a)
> with a small indigo (#4f46e5) gradient app icon. Right side: a polished dashboard
> mockup on a soft off-white background showing a pipeline of four connected cards —
> a resume document, a ranked job list with a green "92% match" badge, a cover-letter
> page, and a kanban tracker. Thin connecting arrows flow left to right between the
> cards. Indigo accent, generous whitespace, subtle drop shadows, flat vector style,
> crisp and professional. No photorealism, no stock-photo people. 1200×627,
> high detail, balanced composition.

---

## Prompt B — Bold "before vs after" concept

> Split-screen tech illustration. Left half (muted, grey, cluttered): a frustrated
> stack of identical "Application" papers being fired into a void labelled "spray &
> pray", with a faint LinkedIn-style ban warning. Right half (bright, indigo + white,
> organized): a clean dashboard with a single tailored application glowing, a green
> match-score badge, and a tidy pipeline tracker. Center title in bold slate:
> "Stop spamming. Start matching." Small tag: "Job Hunter — open source". Flat vector,
> modern SaaS aesthetic, strong contrast between the two halves. 1280×720.

---

## Prompt C — Minimal / icon-led (good if you want it simple)

> Minimalist flat illustration on a soft off-white (#f4f5f8) background. A large indigo
> (#4f46e5) magnifying glass over a resume document, with three small floating cards
> beside it: a green "match %" pill, a cover-letter icon, and a checkmark tracker.
> Bold slate title "JOB HUNTER" and a thin subtitle "resume → ranked jobs → tailored
> applications". Lots of negative space, clean vector lines, professional and calm.
> 1200×627.

---

## Style notes (keep it on-brand with the app)
- **Palette:** deep slate `#0f172a` (text), indigo `#4f46e5` (accent), off-white
  `#f4f5f8` (bg), emerald `#059669` (the "match %" / success green).
- **Font feel:** clean geometric sans (Inter/Söhne-style), bold title.
- **Avoid:** purple-gradient-on-dark "AI slop", generic robot imagery, fake screenshots
  with lorem-ipsum noise, stock photos of people shaking hands.
- **Overlay text option** (if adding in Canva/Figma instead of in-prompt):
  Title "Job Hunter" · Subtitle "Open-source AI job-application engine" · small "★ on GitHub".
