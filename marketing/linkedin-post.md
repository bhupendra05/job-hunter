# LinkedIn post — Job Hunter v1.0 launch

## Primary version

I got tired of two kinds of job-search tools: the ones that just dump listings into a spreadsheet, and the ones that "auto-apply" by spamming 500 employers with the same generic application (and quietly get your LinkedIn flagged).

So I built **Job Hunter** — and open-sourced it. 🛠️

It runs locally and does the 95% that actually lands interviews:

📄 **Profile** — drop in your resume (PDF). Claude reads it and builds a structured profile of your skills, seniority and real target titles.

🎯 **Find & Rank** — pulls live roles from free job boards, then scores every posting against *your* profile: a match %, company-quality tier, why it fits, and the honest gaps.

✍️ **Tailor** — one click writes a specific, human-sounding cover letter for any role, plus resume tweaks and interview talking points for that job.

📌 **Track** — save roles and move them through Saved → Applied → Interviewing → Closed.

No blind auto-submit. No account bans. No 500 spam applications. Just better, faster, tailored applications — with you clicking the final submit.

It's free, open source, and you run it with your own API key. Clone it, point it at your resume, and go.

🔗 Repo + setup in the comments.

If you're job hunting right now, I'd genuinely love your feedback. And a ⭐ on the repo helps more people find it.
